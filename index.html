about

In today’s world, technology plays an important role in every industry as well as in our personal lives. Out of all of the industries, technology plays a crucial role in healthcare which is responsible for improving and saving countless lives all around the world. Flife Foundation strive to build an enthusiastic community to find and crack problems in health sector with emerging technologies. We feel there is a need to solve the problems in medical sector with the help of advanced technology so that it will enhance people’s lives.

Flife Foundation is a non profit organization run by a group of medical and tech enthusiasts with an aim to facilitate technology in medical field to solve problems and foster a healthier society. Our main focus is to use technology to revolutionize health field.

mission

Flife Foundation is keen in identifying issues in medical field. And finding apt solutions using latest technology. Our team will arrange the needed fund for building required solutions through fundraising programs and donation campaigns. Our main focus is to release the product to the public, so that it can be accessible for common people and thereby building a healthier society.

projects

Flife

FLIFE, is a platform which help people in early diagnosis of major diseases with the help of a simple survey through a mobile application. User have to respond to the questions given and based on the responses, a report will be generated and it will clearly state the health condition of the person with respect to the particular disease on which survey have been taken. It helps people to be cautious about their health condition with respect to a particular disease.
